StoriCraft: AI Learning Path Builder

You decide what you want to learn. StoriCraft builds a personalized, structured learning path — just for you. Self-directed, gamified, and powered by AI.

🚧
StoriCraft is in development
We're building a tool that lets any Storian create their own AI-powered learning experience from scratch. No instructional design background needed — just curiosity and a topic you want to master.
Launching Q3 2026
How it will work
1
💡
You pick a topic
Tell StoriCraft what you want to learn — a skill, a concept, a tool, anything relevant to your role or growth.
2
🤖
AI builds your path
StoriCraft generates a structured learning journey — context, key concepts, scenarios, and checkpoints — tailored to you.
3
🎮
You learn and play
Work through your path at your own pace. Earn points, hit milestones, and track your progress as you go.
4
📚
Share with others
Once complete, your path can be added to the Stori learning library for other Storians to discover and take.
What you'll get
🎯
Self-directed learning
You own your path from start to finish. No one prescribes what you learn — you do.
⚡
Built in minutes
No instructional design experience needed. StoriCraft handles the structure — you bring the topic.
🏆
Gamified progress
Points, milestones, and completion badges keep you moving and make learning feel worth finishing.
💬
Conversational learning
The AI talks with you, challenges your thinking, and adapts to how you're doing — not just delivers content.
🔁
Adaptive to you
StoriCraft adjusts depth and pace based on what you already know and how you respond.
🌐
Community library
The best paths get curated into a shared library — one Storian's learning becomes everyone's resource.
Built on three principles
Agency
Learning works best when you choose it. StoriCraft starts with what you want to learn, not what L&D decided you should.
Quality without gatekeeping
AI ensures every path meets a baseline of quality before it reaches others — so the library stays useful without creating bottlenecks.
Learning as contribution
When you build and share a path, you're not just growing yourself — you're adding to Stori's collective intelligence.
